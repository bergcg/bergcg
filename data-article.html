Data-Driven Article using Vega-Lite and D3

  • Objective: Create a data-driven article that seamlessly integrates text and charts to weave a compelling data story, communicating insights from a chosen dataset.
  • Process:
    1. Analyzed raw data to uncover insights and create a well-rounded narrative that users could follow.
    2. Conceptualized the narrative, visualizations, and user interactions through careful design and planning.
    3. Utilized a mix of D3.js and Vega-Lite to create interactive visualizations that highlight key insights and engage the audience.
    4. Added annotations and highlighting to convey the message effectively, ensuring a coordinated text-to-chart presentation.
  • Project Insight: Recognized the potential of technology as a valuable ally in education while advocating for balanced and mindful engagement. Emphasized the importance of collaborative efforts between parents and schools in navigating the digital landscape to nurture children's learning experiences.
  • Outcome: Created a data-driven article that sheds light on how parents perceive digital devices in their children's learning journey, advocating for a balanced integration of technology in education.
  • Skills Demonstrated: Data Cleaning, Data Analysis, Data Visualization (Vega-Lite, D3), Narrative Crafting, User Research, Insight Generation.
