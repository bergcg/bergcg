Exploration and Exposition with Tableau

  • Objective: Explore and communicate intriguing and significant aspects within the dataset.
  • Process:
    1. Cleaned the dataset and formulated questions to guide the exploration, aiming to understand the extent of parental engagement facilitated by technology.
    2. Explored relationships between multiple variables through iterative cycles of questioning and visual analysis, uncovering compelling insights.
    3. Transformed insights into a coherent narrative in the Exposition phase, utilizing three easy-to-follow dashboards.
  • Findings: The analysis revealed the significant role of technology in connecting parents and providing deeper insights into their children's progress. Dashboards showcased how this information empowers parents to support their children's academic success actively.
  • Skills Demonstrated: Data Exploration, Data Visualization (Tableau), Insight Generation, Narrative Crafting, Dashboard Design, Annotation and Highlighting Techniques.
