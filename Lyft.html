Elevating Lyft's Profit Potential

  • Objective: Analyze the rideshare market dynamics to evaluate Lyft's position relative to its main competitor, Uber, and provide actionable recommendations for strategic improvement.
  • Process:
    1. Conducted thorough secondary research by gathering information from market reports and industry analyses to understand the market landscape.
    2. Designed and conducted focus groups and surveys to collect primary data directly from consumers, focusing on their preferences and perceptions regarding various aspects of rideshare services.
    3. Analyzed collected data using descriptive and inferential statistics, as well as predictive analysis techniques to forecast potential market trends.
  • User Insights: Revealed a significant gap in consumer awareness between Lyft and Uber, despite offering similar features. Consumers showed a preference for Uber, citing better brand recognition and perceived reliability.
  • Project Insight: Highlighting and refining Lyft's brand image, enhancing its marketing efforts, and transparently communicating its competitive advantages are crucial for closing the awareness gap and improving market position.
  • Outcome: Developed a comprehensive report with actionable recommendations for Lyft to refine its brand image, enhance marketing efforts, and communicate its competitive advantages more transparently. This data-driven approach provided strategic insights to inform decision-making in a competitive market environment.
  • Skills Demonstrated: Team Collaboration, Secondary Research, Survey Design (Qualtrics), Data Collection, Descriptive and Inferential Statistics (SPSS), Predictive Analysis, Strategic Marketing Recommendations, Data-Driven Decision Making.
